Introduction

“Happy Youth Sabbath, everyone! I am not here to entertain you. I am here to remind you who you are. You are not here by accident. You were placed in this generation on purpose. God could have placed you in the time of the pioneers… but He chose this generation — the generation living in the final hour.”

“The world is confusing. Pressure is everywhere. Temptation is everywhere. Distraction is part of our daily life. But God is calling you to stand, to shine, and to finish the work He started centuries ago.”

Today is Youth Sabbath. We come as the generation You have chosen.

Touch every young person here. Strengthen, revive, and prepare us to be part of the final generation.

Children’s Illustration – “The Phone With No Signal”

“Imagine the most expensive, fastest phone ever. Latest updates. Amazing features. But no signal. It cannot work. Many of us are like this spiritually. We look connected — but our connection to God is dead.”
Youth Examples:
- Scrolling TikTok/Instagram 5–8 hours/day
- Sleeping late, missing Sabbath worship
- Knowing trends but not Scripture

“The Three Angels’ Message calls us to reconnect. Let’s open Revelation 14.”

Revelation 14 – Our Prophetic Identity

Fear God: Respect God more than peer pressure.

Youth example: Choosing truth over gossip, refusing to cheat.
Proverbs 1:7 – “The fear of the Lord is the beginning of knowledge.”

Give God Glory: Live publicly & privately for God.

Example: Social media posts showing faith, not trends.
1 Corinthians 10:31 – “Whatever you do, do it all for the glory of God.”

Worship the Creator: Sabbath, devotion, lifestyle.

Example: Attending Sabbath joyfully, not casually.
Exodus 20:8 – “Remember the Sabbath day, to keep it holy.”

Come Out of Babylon: Reject wrong pressures.

Example: Music with bad messages, peer pressure, toxic friends.
“Babylon today si kibuga kyokka… Babylon is a mindset. Embeera y’ensi eraga nti truth is optional. But Katonda takyusa truth ye.”

Real-life tie-in: Music, TikTok trends, school pressures, peer pressure, gaming culture — all modern Babylon.

Daniel – Standing Under Pressure

Read Daniel 1:8. Daniel refused royal food to honor God. He stood as a youth under pressure.

Youth examples today:
- Peer pressure to cheat in exams
- Pressure to try alcohol or drugs
- Pressure to date in wrong ways
- Fitting in online vs standing for God
“Daniel yagamba nti, ‘Nze sirye ku mmere eno.’ Babylon yali ya maanyi. Naye Katonda yali wa maanyi okusinga.”
1 Timothy 4:12 – “Let no one despise your youth, but set an example in speech, conduct, love, faith, and purity.”

Character in the Final Crisis

Final events will test loyalty.

James 1:12 – “Blessed is the one who stays strong under trial.”
Revelation 14:12 – “Here is the patience of the saints, those who keep God’s commandments and faith in Jesus.”
Youth character tips:
- Turn off phone during prayer/devotion
- Write down your spiritual victories
- Choose friends who encourage faith
- Keep purity in dating, speech, and social media
“Your private choices show who you really are. Are you a Daniel in secret?”

Final Call – Stand Now
